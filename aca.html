We offer Both CBC and 8-4-4 systems

CBC System

The 8-4-4 system is gradually phased out because it was deemed unsuitable for the changing aspirations of Kenyans and the labour market which was slowly beginning to embrace technology. We therefore offer upto date CBC training in our school.

8-4-4 System

The learners that were already in course when CBC was introduced are also gathered for in our school. We offer them the best learning environments to enable them produce the best in their final year.
